Frequently Asked Questions

What is Micro-SaaS?
Micro-SaaS is a specialized software service focused on solving specific domain problems. These services are typically operated by individuals or small teams, characterized by low maintenance costs but high profit margins. Due to its small scale and focused nature, it's ideal for side projects or small startup ventures. This model allows entrepreneurs to enter the market with minimal risk while maintaining flexibility and efficiency.
Why Choose Streamlit?
Streamlit is a highly advantageous development framework that offers an extremely low entry barrier, making it perfect for rapid business idea validation. It fully supports the Python ecosystem and provides rich built-in data visualization tools. More importantly, Streamlit offers automatic responsive design, allowing developers to create professional web applications without frontend development experience. Additionally, its deployment process is straightforward and supports various cloud service integrations, significantly reducing development and maintenance complexity.
Why Integrate Patreon for Membership and Payment Management?
Choosing to integrate Patreon as our membership and payment management system is a strategic decision. This approach allows us to avoid developing complex payment systems while gaining access to a mature subscription mechanism. Patreon provides comprehensive international payment solutions and membership management backend, significantly reducing development and maintenance costs while ensuring system stability and security. By utilizing an existing mature platform, we can focus more energy on core product development.
What Technical Background is Required?
To start using our system, you need basic Python programming skills and a fundamental understanding of HTML/CSS. Basic database knowledge is also helpful for the development process. However, the most important thing is having the passion and drive for continuous learning, as technology is constantly evolving. Our platform's design philosophy is to lower technical barriers, making development accessible to more people. Even as a beginner, you can gradually master the necessary skills if you're willing to learn.
